About Us

Who we are


We are a team of young students who are inspired to make our world a better place, we cannot accomplish this goal alone, so we want to create a website that gathers brief information related to scholarships, competitions, trainings, exchange programs, and a variety of events for students and youth to explore, experience and get the opportunity to better themselves. Our website welcomes individuals of any age but mainly targets young and bright minded individuals that seek to make our world a better place.

Vision


At our current state of our project, we are first limiting the reach to only in Cambodia and will spread our roots when our project will continue to succeed!
To make things exciting, we will be hosting numerous events and competitions of our own, and to make things more exciting and publicly available to all young participants.
EVERYONE will be able to join FREE of charge!

Mission


Our mission is to share important and useful information and events so that the people from our young generation have the chance and opportunity to shine and reach an inspiring goal.
